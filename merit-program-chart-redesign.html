Problem and Solution

  • Problem: Members of Circle K International at Sac State had to read a confusing, ambiguous chart to understand the requirements for bronze, silver, gold, and platinum-tier recognition in the club. The chart is especially confusing for new members if no explanation is provided.
  • Solution: A revised interactive chart with more articulate explanations of tier requirements

Skills Demonstrated

  • Technical skills: Google Sheets
  • Soft skills: Organizational skills, collaboration, task-focused communication, clear explanation

Results

  • The new chart was praised by the other CKI board members for its improved usability

Description

As the tech chair for the Circle K International club at Sac State, I saw an opportunity to improve the existing Member Recognition Program (MRP) chart, which I immediately noticed was somewhat vague and confusing. Working with the other board members, I made a revised version with clarifying details and interactive checkboxes that members can use to check which tiers they qualify for.
